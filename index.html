Quickly measure your knuckle width to determine your perfect bangle size.

1. Squeeze Hand Tightly

Tuck your thumb inward toward your palm, squeezing your knuckles as if sliding a real bangle on.

2. Keep Hand Flat & Still

Place your hand flat on a table in a well-lit area. Avoid tilting your palm to ensure accurate depth mapping.

3. Level Phone Directly Above

Hover your phone parallel to your hand. Keep the camera stationary for 1.5 seconds to calibrate and lock the size.

Requires an Android device with Google Play Services for AR (ARCore) in Google Chrome.
