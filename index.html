How to Measure

1. Squeeze Hand Tightly

Tuck your thumb inward and squeeze your knuckles tight, as if sliding a real bangle on.

2. Keep Hand Flat & Still

Place your hand flat on a table in a well-lit area. Avoid tilting your palm for accurate mapping.

3. Level Phone Directly Above

Hold phone parallel directly above your hand and keep still for 1.5 seconds to calibrate.
